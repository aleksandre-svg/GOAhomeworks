Meet the Characters

Po

Po is the main character, a lovable and clumsy panda who becomes the Dragon Warrior.

Tigress

Tigress is a strong and skilled fighter who initially doubts Po but later becomes his trusted ally.

Monkey

Monkey is a quick-witted, fun-loving member of the Furious Five, with incredible agility.

Crane

Crane is a calm and strategic fighter who uses his flight abilities to help his team in battle.

Viper

Viper is a gentle and wise member of the Furious Five, known for her precision and agility.

Mantis

Mantis may be small, but he's incredibly strong and fast, making him an important member of the team.
