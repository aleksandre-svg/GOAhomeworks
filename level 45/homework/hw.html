Programming Academy: Pros & Cons

Pluses

  • Great for structured learning.
  • Access to experienced mentors.
  • Online learning System
  • Big future
  • Friendly and lovely community

Minuses

  • Can be a bit hard for someones.
  • May not offer enough hands-on practice.
  • Too much control
  • May not be flexible with your schedule.
  • Can feel too rigid or structured.
