All About Pineapples

Pineapples are tropical fruits known for their sweet and tangy flavor. They are rich in vitamins and minerals, particularly vitamin C and manganese.

Pineapples grow on a plant with spiky green leaves and a tough outer skin. The flesh of the fruit is juicy and bright yellow.

They are used in various culinary dishes, from fruit salads to juices, and are a key ingredient in many tropical recipes.

The pineapple is native to South America, and it is now grown in many tropical and subtropical regions of the world.
